Who I Am

My name is Tom, a graduate Web Designer, achieving a 1st class honours degree at Edge Hill University in Web Design & Development.

I am 33 years old and returning to education after 10+ years working various roles within the construction and logistics industries.

Taking the technical and theoretical skills that I have learned during my study, in addition to a wealth of real world experience, I hope to transition a passion for the web into a career.

I pride myself on my problem solving and logical thinking which equips me perfectly to make the web better and more accessible for everyone.

Feel free to navigate to my projects page or use my social links to see some of my work. Alternatively, you can contact me directly to discuss any queries you may have.

Who I Really Am

As I am sure comes with the territory in the web field, I avidly consume anything pertaining to computers. In my spare time I enjoy playing a wide variety of video games, watching streams and youtube content and generally broadening my horizons through social media, discussion boards and online course content.
